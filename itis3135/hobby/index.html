About

I have been lifting weights for 2.5 years, but just recently started taking powerlifting more seriously. Within those 2 years I gained around 30 lbs of muscle, and progressed all of my lifts upwards of 200 lbs. The excersises I focus on the most are bench press, squat, and the conventional deadlift. I got into lifting when covid hit in 2019 and since then have been consistently going to the gym 6 days per week.

Current Maxes:

  • Bench Press: 265 lbs
  • Squat: 365 lbs
  • Deadlift: 475 lbs
