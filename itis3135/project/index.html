Welcome to Military Source!

Here you will find information, history and news about each military branch, so you can be kept up to date about what is happening in our country.



The US military consists of 5 main branches which are the Army, Air Force, Navy, Coast Guard, and Marines. Each branch serves a special purpose in protecting, defending, and maintaining freedom within our nation. The military was established in 1775, and has played a major role in the development of our nation. Within this website, you will find information regarding the rich history, current status, and qualifications/careers for each branch!
